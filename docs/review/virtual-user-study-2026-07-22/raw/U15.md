## Severity-tagged issues

### P1 — Persona level mismatch

Onboarding resulted in Intermediate path for an Advanced persona; no visible level choice was observed.

### P1 — Start Learning transition appeared unreliable

After clicking Start Learning, the screen initially remained unchanged; a later click timed out, then a fresh snapshot showed navigation to Tracks. Needs reproducible follow-up.

### P2 — Content wording/formatting quality

Topic heading included literal backticks around `useReducer`; objectives displayed lowercase `usestate`/`usereducer`; some prompts repeated full topic titles unnaturally.

### P2 — Developer-tools affordance always present

“Open Next.js Dev Tools” appeared across learner screens and may distract from the learning task.

No P0 confirmed.

## Language and accessibility

English UI was readable and structurally labeled. Arabic selection/translation/input/search and RTL were not reached. Keyboard-only and screen-reader behavior were not reached; contrast/focus were only visually spot-checked.

## Confidence / limitations / continue decision

Confidence is partial: landing, onboarding, Home, Tracks, and R-M07 Topic 1 are evidenced; all remaining assigned flows and shared surfaces lack evidence. Continue testing; do not certify release readiness from this partial run.
